01 — About me

My introduction

I am an experienced HVAC/ACMV draftsman and Mechanical BIM Modeler creating detailed schematic drawings, shop drawings and as-built documentation using Revit and AutoCAD — covering HVAC, firefighting, LPG and water supply services.

I develop accurate 2D and 3D BIM models (LOD 100 & 300) in compliance with project BIM Execution Plans, build Revit project templates, run clash detection in Navisworks Manage, and coordinate with architects, structural and MEP teams to deliver clash-free models.

Beyond modeling, I conduct internal model audits and QC checks, manage drawing revisions, and train and mentor junior staff in Revit, Navisworks and project workflows — across projects in the UAE, KSA, Australia and India.
